Blueberry balls

These are an easy & healthy snack or quick breaksfast!

  • Ingredients
  • How to

Require minimal ingredients, are super quick to make and are a good snack for toddlers and adults.

Ingredients:

  • 150 g of frozen bluberries
  • 160 g of rolled oats
  • 35 g of coconut
  • 1 tbs of vanilla extract

How to:

  1. Add all of the ingredients in a food processor
  2. Add the extra coconut to a bowl and then form balls
  3. Store in the fridge

Bon apetit!
